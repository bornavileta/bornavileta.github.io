Welcome

Website of an 18-year-old ICT student at HZ University of Applied Science

Here you will learn about me and the choices I made to lead me here.

My goal here is to become a great software developer and master many programming languages. My journey at HBO-ICT actually did not start this year; it started in 2021. After finishing my high school I had only just turned 17 years old. You could say I was pretty clueless about in which direction I wanted to take my life. As a kid I was always interested in tech (especially phones and laptops). So I thought to myself: why not ICT? That has to do with computers and stuff... right?

Well I quickly found out that coding and programming actually IS as difficult as it seems. But I didn't mind it. I kept on trying, struggling, failing and finding solutions. But as time went on, everything got harder. I got distracted easier and I lost my focus. I fell behind and lost all my motivation very quickly. So i quit. I told myself: there must be another study that suits me better. So I started searching. Many months of research later, I found myself back at square one: nothing peaked my interest as much as ICT did. That realisation completed the cycle, and brought me back here.
