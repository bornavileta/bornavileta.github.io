How do you print a document here at HZ?

You click 'print' on the desired document and then you select one of the HZ printers. You walk to the printer you selected, scan your HZ card, click 'print', select the documents you want to have printed and wait for them to print.

How do you scan a document and send it to your laptop here at HZ?

You scan your HZ card on one of the printers and select 'scan' on the screen of the printer. Then you select 'scan & send' and put your documents in the printer to scan them. After that, you click the green button and the documents will be scanned and sent to your HZ e-mail adress.

What to do when you have COVID-19 symptomes?

If this is the case, you'll have to follow the lessons online untill you are healthy again.

How can you reserve a project room in one of the wings of the building?

You can do this online on the HZ website. These rooms can be reserved by everyone, so sometimes you'll have to be quick. The rooms are quiet places for you and other students to study in.

What are the instructions to park my carat the HZ parking lot?

You just follow the instructions on the signs when you drive into the parking lot.
