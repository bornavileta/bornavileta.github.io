Frequently Asked Questions

How do I print a document at HZ?
  • First make sure you have enough money on your HZ pass
  • Go to print.hz.nl
  • Log in with your HZ username and password
  • Click on 'choose file' and select the file you want to print
  • Select the specific options you want to apply
  • Select the printer you want to use
  • Scan your HZ card at the card reader next to the selected printer
  • Select the option Print / Afdrukken
How do I scan and save a document to my laptop?
  • Scan your HZ card at the card reader next to a printer
  • Select the option 'scan'
  • Put the paper you want to scan in on the glass plate
  • Click on 'scan and send' and press the start button
  • Now press the green start button on the printer
  • The document will now be scanned in and sent to your HZ mail
How do I buy something at the HZ webshop?
  • Go to webshop.hz.nl
  • Select a room and fill in your details
  • Complete the reservation
Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do I show symptoms of the coronavirus?

How do I park my car at HZ?
  • In Vlissingen, the parking lot is right in front of the school building. When you press your HZ card against the reader on the pole, the gate will open
  • In Middelburg, you have to drive up to the following location: Poelendaelesingel, 4335 JA Middelburg There, you can also press your HZ card against the reader to open the gate and park your car
